Summary: This article presents a systematic analysis of the global, regional, and national burden of surgical site infection (SSI) from 1990 to 2021, conducted for the Global Burden of Disease Study 2021. It highlights SSIs as a major cause of morbidity and mortality, emphasizing their significant health and economic impact worldwide. The study aims to provide comprehensive data to inform and improve prevention strategies and patient outcomes globally.
