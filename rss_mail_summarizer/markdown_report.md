- This article explains Fortune's Algorithm, a method for generating Voronoi Diagrams in O(nlogn) time, which partitions a plane into regions based on the distance to a set of points called sites. The algorithm uses a sweep line and a beachline of parabolic arcs to determine the edges and vertices of the Voronoi diagram, with interesting events occurring when the sweep line encounters a site or when arcs are squeezed out of existence. The article also covers key concepts like site events, circle events, incomplete edges, and the Doubly Connected Edge List (DCEL) data structure for storing the Voronoi diagram. [(click here)](https://redpenguin101.github.io/html/posts/2025_01_21_voronoi.html)
