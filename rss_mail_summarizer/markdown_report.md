# News of the Day

## Carlo Masala

- NATO allies Poland and Romania are strengthening their air defenses following recent drone incidents near their borders, particularly after a Russian drone crashed in Romania. While NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg emphasized that there is no indication of an intentional Russian attack, the incidents highlight the risks of the Ukraine war spilling over into NATO territory. Both countries are increasing surveillance and defense measures, with Romania deploying F-16 jets and Poland considering additional air defense systems.
