## Business and Finance

- Bybit, a Dubai-based exchange, was recently the victim of a massive cryptocurrency theft, with approximately $1.5 billion stolen, likely by North Korean actors. The attackers transferred the funds from a Multisig Cold Wallet to a hot wallet before moving them out of Bybit altogether. Multisig cold wallets are usually a gold standard for security, but the attackers were still able to bypass this security measure. [(click here)](https://arstechnica.com/security/2025/02/how-north-korea-pulled-off-a-1-5-billion-crypto-heist-the-biggest-in-history/)
- Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, is urging for a more realistic view of AI, emphasizing the need for measurable economic growth rather than chasing artificial general intelligence (AGI). He suggests that AI's value should be judged by its ability to drive significant productivity increases and economic expansion, similar to the Industrial Revolution. Despite substantial investments in AI, Nadella acknowledges that current AI technologies have not yet demonstrated substantial real-world value. [(click here)](https://futurism.com/microsoft-ceo-ai-generating-no-value)
